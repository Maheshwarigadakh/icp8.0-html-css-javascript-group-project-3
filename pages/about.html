Our story...

Since 1954, Jewelry Palace has quietly delighted the connoisseurs of Bangalore with it's breathtaking designs, intricate craftsmanship & ancient techniques.

Every Jewelry Palace pan India contains a wealth of diamonds, gold & platinum jewellery. Be It faceted diamonds, jadau or meenakari, Navrathan strives to create antique with each of its pieces.
